Instructions for Spearmint OS 0.2

In order for Spearmint OS to work, your computer needs to be turned off and booted into the optical disk. To begin, restart your computer. Then, during the boot process repeatedly press the boot key on your computer, then select the CD/DVD option in the boot menu. You can find the boot key for your model computer in the table below.
